Get to know us

We are a Design Engineering Service company established in 2024 and provide full-service in BIM MEP using specialized software. We perform MEP studies (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Fighting and Specialized Systems), design and Modelling, Workshop drawing, Revit modulation, design review and validation, equipment selection, As-built drawings ,planning and coordination in buildings and constructions.

Our Mission

To become the leading, reliable MEP Engineering and consultant firm with 25 Engineers or more, providing sustainable solutions from simple to complex design services in Europe, Middle East, and India.


Our Vision

Our mission is to provide quality solutions that add value to our environment and society, ensuring reliability and customer confidence, and to supply services that meet the requirements of its customers at all times.
